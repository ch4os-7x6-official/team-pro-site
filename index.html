Getting Started

Follow these steps to get up and running.

1
Get an Executor
Download a Roblox script executor such as Synapse X, KRNL, or any other supported executor.
2
Copy the Loadstring
Head to the Scripts section and copy the loadstring for the version you want.
3
Inject and Execute
Open Roblox, inject your executor, paste the loadstring into the executor and hit execute.
4
Enter Your Key (Main only)
If using Pro GUI (main), complete the key system to unlock all features. The key lasts 24 hours. Pro GUI Lite requires no key.
5
Open the GUI
The GUI will appear after loading. Use the Pro GUI button in the bottom left to reopen it if closed.
